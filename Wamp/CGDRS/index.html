City sights

The Jet d'Eau fountain

in Geneva

Date: April 2003

The Jet d'Eau fountain is the most recognizeable symbol of Geneva. The fountain reaches 140 meters (460 feet) high, roughly the same height as the Embassy Suites hotel in Times Square.


Double-decker bus

in London

Date: October 2002

The unescapable symbol of London, the double-decker bus is much taller than typical buses to carry many passagers through London overcrowded streets.


Eiffel tower

in Paris

Date: May 2003

The Eiffel tower was built for the International Exhibition of Paris of 1900. It's a sight you can't miss, it is shown here from an RER train station.


Coliseum

in Roma

Date: February 2003

The Coliseum is regarded by many as one of the finest example of Roman engineering. It hosted public games where criminals, slaves and animals fought each other until death.
